# Evercrew Philosophy

## Why this project exists

Many SMEs know AI is useful.

Few know how to implement it safely, practically, and affordably.

Many existing AI projects are designed for developers.

This project is designed for business owners.

Our goal is to demonstrate simple, practical AI workflows that SMEs can understand, install, and improve.

---

## Our Principles

### Local First

Whenever practical, AI should run locally.

Businesses should have the choice to keep their own data on their own computers.

---

### Human First

AI should assist humans.

AI should not replace business judgement.

Important actions should always allow human confirmation.

---

### Simplicity

Simple solutions are easier to maintain.

Simple solutions are easier to trust.

Simple solutions are easier to teach.

---

### Practical AI

We do not build AI because it is fashionable.

We build AI because it solves real business problems.

Every workflow should save time, reduce repetitive work, or improve business productivity.

---

### Community

We believe practical AI becomes better when shared.

We welcome ideas, improvements, bug reports, and contributions from the community.

---

## Long-term Vision

To become the most practical open-source local AI workflow ecosystem for SMEs.
