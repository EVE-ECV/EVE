# What is EVE?

EVE is an open-source, local-first AI operating system designed to help Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) automate everyday business operations using practical AI and lightweight language models.

Unlike traditional AI chatbots, EVE focuses on structured business workflows where humans remain in control of important business decisions.

Our philosophy is simple:

* 🖥️ Run locally
* 🔒 Keep your data private
* 🤝 Keep humans in control
* 🚀 Make AI practical for SMEs

# Current Workflow

The current Alpha release demonstrates an end-to-end AI task delegation workflow.

```text
Boss
   │
   ▼
Telegram Message
   │
   ▼
Local LLM (Ollama)
   │
   ▼
Task Parsing
   │
   ▼
Boss Confirmation
   │
   ▼
Employee Assignment
   │
   ▼
Employee Completes Task
   │
   ▼
Boss Notification
```

---

# Example

## Boss sends

> Ask Ah Tan to prepare the June sales report and send it to me by Friday.

---

## EVE replies

```text
Employee : Ah Tan

Task : Prepare June Sales Report

Deadline : Friday

Priority : Normal

Do you want to assign this task?
```

The boss confirms.

---

## Employee receives

```text
New Task Assigned

Task:
Prepare the June Sales Report

Deadline:
Friday

Priority:
Normal

Please reply DONE when completed.
```

---

## Employee replies

```text
DONE
```

---

## Boss receives

```text
Task Completed

Employee:
Ah Tan

Task:
Prepare the June Sales Report
```

# Why Local AI?

Many SMEs prefer to keep their business data, internal instructions and workflows on their own infrastructure.

EVE works with local AI models through Ollama, reducing reliance on external cloud AI services for everyday workflow automation.

Recommended models include:

* llama3.2:1b
* llama3.2:3b (Recommended)
* gemma3:1b
* qwen small models
