# EVE Project Principles

## Our Vision

EVE (Evercrew Virtual Engine) aims to become a practical, local-first AI operating system that helps small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) automate real business workflows while keeping humans in control.

---

# Our Principles

## 1. Human Before AI

AI should assist people, not replace them.

EVE always encourages human review and confirmation before important business actions are performed.

---

## 2. Local First

Whenever practical, EVE is designed to run entirely on local infrastructure.

Businesses should have the choice to keep sensitive operational data within their own environment.

---

## 3. Privacy by Design

Privacy is not an optional feature.

EVE minimizes unnecessary data collection and encourages local processing whenever possible.

---

## 4. Workflow Before Conversation

Most businesses need workflows, not endless conversations.

EVE focuses on helping people complete work rather than simply generating text.

---

## 5. Practical AI

We value AI that solves everyday business problems.

Simple, reliable automation is often more valuable than highly complex AI systems.

---

## 6. Small Models First

Where possible, EVE is designed to work well with efficient local language models.

Larger models may be supported, but they should not be a requirement for everyday business automation.

---

## 7. Modular by Design

Every capability in EVE should be developed as a reusable module.

Examples include:

- Telegram
- WhatsApp
- Email
- HR
- Sales
- Customer Service
- Finance

Modules should work independently while sharing the same workflow engine.

---

## 8. Open Standards

Whenever practical, EVE adopts open standards, open-source software, and interoperable technologies.

---

## 9. Built for SMEs

Every design decision should answer one question:

> Does this genuinely help SMEs become more productive?

If not, it probably does not belong in EVE.

---

## 10. Continuous Improvement

EVE is an evolving open-source project.

We welcome constructive feedback, community contributions, and practical ideas that improve the platform for everyone.

---

# Our Mission

Empower businesses with practical AI that is secure, understandable, and accessible.
